Welcome to Little Smile Foundation

Helping children smile again through care, hope and support.

Our Story

The Little Smile Foundation began with a vision to fight child poverty and provide hope to children living in disadvantaged communities. From rural Australia to countries like Bangladesh, India, and Kenya, we work tirelessly to ensure that every child has access to food, healthcare, and education.

Our Mission

To provide sustainable support that addresses hunger, health, and education for children in need worldwide.

Our Vision

A world where every child grows up with opportunities, dignity, and the chance to smile.
